Meet Kelvin

Kelvin is a dedicated professional engineer and interior designer with over 10 years of experience serving clients across Kenya. His passion for precision engineering and creative design has established him as a trusted expert in the industry.

Born and raised in Nairobi, Kelvin understands the unique architectural and design needs of Kenyan homes and businesses. He combines technical expertise with cultural sensitivity to deliver solutions that are both functional and beautiful.
